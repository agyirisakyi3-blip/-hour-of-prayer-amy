Welcome to Hour Of Prayer Amy

Warm Christian greetings to you, and welcome to Hour Of Prayer Amy! We are a place where God is saving, healing and delivering souls from the bondage of sin.

In a day and age when the Word of God is being watered-down, it is good to know that there is a people reaching out to the world with Life Transforming Truth.

Our doors are always open. Please join us for our weekly services and experience the transformative power of God's love.

"For God so loved the world that He gave His only begotten Son, that whoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life." - John 3:16
